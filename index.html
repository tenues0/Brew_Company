Freedom in Brewing!

The Tashlitsky Beer Company started as a hobby in Monterey California as a hobby for Teddy during breaks at school. He started brewing one gallon beers kits and eventually went on to brewing five gallon kits. Upon graduation from NPS, he moved back down to Oceanside California with Xenia and began brewing beer with his friend Chris. Chris had experience brewing all grain recipes. Chris also had access to Beer Smith software where he mentored Teddy on how to build a recipe. The Tashlitsky Beer Company is a continuation of Teddy's education in making beer and creating a website to go along with it. We hope you enjoy our craft.
